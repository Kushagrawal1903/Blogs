## About the Book

The Developer's Playbook is the book I wish I had when I started my journey in tech. It's not just about writing code — it's about thinking like an engineer, communicating effectively, and building systems that matter.

## What's Inside

### Part 1: Foundations
- The engineering mindset
- Learning how to learn
- Building your first real project

### Part 2: Technical Depth
- Data structures and algorithms that actually matter
- System design principles
- Security-first development

### Part 3: Career Growth
- Building your personal brand
- Open source contributions
- Interview preparation strategies

### Part 4: Beyond Code
- Technical writing
- Team collaboration
- Staying current in a fast-moving industry

## Who Is This For

This book is for anyone starting their journey in software development — students, career changers, and self-taught developers who want a structured path forward.

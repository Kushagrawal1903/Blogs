## About the Book

Fundamentals of Internet of Things is a complete introduction to the world of connected devices. From sensors and microcontrollers to cloud platforms and data analytics, this book covers the full IoT stack with clarity and practical examples.

## What's Inside

### Part 1: IoT Foundations
- What is the Internet of Things
- IoT architecture and protocols
- Sensors, actuators, and embedded systems

### Part 2: Connectivity & Communication
- Wireless communication standards
- MQTT, CoAP, and HTTP protocols
- Edge computing fundamentals

### Part 3: Real-World Applications
- Smart home systems
- Industrial IoT use cases
- Healthcare and agriculture IoT

### Part 4: Security & Future
- IoT security challenges
- Privacy considerations
- The future of connected systems

## Who Is This For

This book is for students, hobbyists, and professionals who want to understand IoT from the ground up , no prior hardware experience required.
